Founders

Founded in 2003 by Boba "Fett" Wozniak and Theo Vlad Tepes in London, Netcode was created with hopes of increasing the workforce in the domain of networks

The problem at the time was bringing interest to this domain, mainly to children, as they were the ones who would hold the keys to the future of networks

History

  • 2003: founded
  • 2005: organized their first annual networking/programming competition
  • 2006, november: created various developping software for networks, including netsim
  • 2008: released edunet, an educational version of netsim, which flopped
  • 2009: 2nd educational software in the form of an MMROPG : netkingdom.
  • 2012: netkingdom is now free to the general public.
  • 2014: Partenered with Cisco
  • 2015: First module compatible with Cisco equipement
  • 2018: First coding classes in partnership with Cisco
